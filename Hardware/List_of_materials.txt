List of Components or Parts 
• Microcontroller: ESP32 (30-pin Dev Kit) 
• Voltage Regulator: LM2596  
• Display: OLED Display  
• Audio Amplifier: MAX98357A (I2S Mono Amp) 
• User Inputs: 4x Momentary Push Buttons (Volume+, Volume-, Channel+, Channel-) 
• Power Input: 2-pin Terminal Block (for External Power)
